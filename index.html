Solar Energy

Solar energy is the energy from the sun that is converted into thermal or electrical energy with the help of photovoltaic cells.It is also pollution-free because there are no harmful gases, chemicals and fly ash produced and also the cleanest and most abundant renewable energy source available

Hydro Energy

Hydroelectric power is produced with the flow of water, usually from an elevation change.It is one of the oldest sources of energy used to turn paddle wheels on rivers.

Geothermal Energy

Geothermal energy is the energy that comes from the heat below the surface of the earth.To access this energy, wells are dug deep into underground reservoirs to access steam and hot water present there, which can be used to drive turbines and produce electricity.

Wind Energy

Wind energy is the energy in which the wind is used to generate mechanical power or electricity. It is a clean and reliable source of renewable energy. Wind turbines convert the kinetic energy in the wind into mechanical power.
